Retreat to a First Class Accomodation

Emirates Palace

Set on 85 hectares of landscaped gardens, this palatial beachfront hotel is 4 km from both the Marina Mall and the Qasr al-Hosn fort. The opulent rooms feature balconies with garden, pool or gulf views. All provide free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Suites add living rooms and whirlpool tubs. Room and butler service are offered. There are 14 restaurants and bars, including a romantic BBQ eatery on the beach. Other amenities include adventure and leisure pools, a kids' club, and jogging and cycling trails, plus a spa and 2 fitness centres. The hotel has a private beach and a marina. Bike rentals, yoga sessions and camel rides are available.
The Ritz-Carlton Abu Dhabi

Set in a grand Venetian-style building, this luxe beachfront resort with sprawling gardens is 3 km from the ornate Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque. Accommodations range from chic rooms with balconies with water views, to sleek 2-bedroom villas with butler service and kitchens. All have Wi-Fi (fee), flat-screen TVs and Nespresso machines, plus marble bathrooms and high-end linens. Upgraded quarters add access to a lounge with free Wi-Fi. Room service is 24/7. Parking and a breakfast buffet are free. Amenities include 8 restaurants/bars with Italian and refined Asian options, a private beach and a 1,600-sq-m pool. There's a spa, as well as a gym and a ballroom.
Park Hyatt Abu Dhabi Hotels and Villas

Set on the beach overlooking the Arabian Gulf, this posh hotel is 5 km from Saadiyat Beach Golf Club. Sleek rooms feature balconies, free Wi-Fi and flat-screens, as well as Nespresso machines, minibars, and marble bathrooms with soaking tubs and rain showers. Suites add living rooms, while villas have kitchens and massage tables. Upgraded quarters add beach views, complimentary breakfast and plunge pools. There are 3 restaurants, 2 bars, and a cafe serving afternoon tea, plus 4 outdoor pools, a hot tub and a spa. Other amenities include a gym, a tennis court and a kids' club. Boat tours of coastal habitats are available (fee).
